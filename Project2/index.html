Women Writers from the Middle East and North Africa

A repository

This project highlights women writers from the Middle East and North Africa. It serves as a compilation of names of women writers, who should be read and celebrated for their brilliant work and their resistance. This project is a reminder for everyone to read their works, a testamant to their talent, strengh and resiliency. This project is intended to be an open-source one where people can submit suggestions for names to be included. If you want to do so, please fill out this form and I will follow-up with you.
